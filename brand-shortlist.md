# Brand Shortlist — Values-Driven Homeschool Family Matching

## Current favorite

# 1. Gadugy

**Positioning:** A trusted local community for families trying to grow children in good soil — with shared values, education hopes, and family rhythms.

**Why it works:**
- Traditional and rooted
- Warm and wholesome
- Subtly faith-friendly without being explicitly denominational
- Strong metaphor for children, formation, community, and long-term growth
- Works for ages 0–9 and homeschool/pre-homeschool families

**Possible domain:**
- `gadugy.com` — purchased by Jimmy as the primary domain

**Tagline options:**
- Meet local families growing in the same direction.
- Find nearby families who share your hopes for your children’s future.
- A private community for values-driven homeschool families.

# Additional Strong Options to Consider

## 6. Rooted Kin

**Why it fits:**

**Possible tagline:**

---

## 7. Homeward Circle

**Positioning:** A warm family circle for homeschool and home-centered families.

**Why it fits:**
- Prior candidate with strong emotional resonance
- “Homeward” implies a return to home, family, education, and rootedness
- Gentle, mainstream, non-combative

**Possible tagline:**
> Find nearby families on a similar path.

# Naming Criteria

The name should feel:
- Traditional and rooted
- Warm and wholesome
- Trustworthy for parents
- Values-driven without sounding combative
- Broad enough to include homeschooling, preparing to homeschool, and alternative-education families
- Serious enough for ID verification and curated matching
- Gentle enough for families with young children

# Current recommendation

Keep **Gadugy** as the primary working name while generating at least two other serious contenders. The next availability step is registrar-level domain search and trademark/social-handle screening before purchase.

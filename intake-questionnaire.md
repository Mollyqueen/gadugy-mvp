# Gadugy — Early Access + Matching Intake v1

## Intake Design Principle

The intake should feel thoughtful and protective, not invasive. Use progressive disclosure:

1. Short early access form
2. Deeper family profile
3. Visibility/privacy controls
4. Verification after value is clear

# Gadugy MVP

A standalone MVP for values-driven Los Angeles homeschool and preparing-to-homeschool families with children ages 0–9 to find nearby families who share their hopes for their children's future.

## Current working brand

Primary: **Gadugy**

Domain: **gadugy.com**

Meaning: **Gadugy** is a Cherokee word meaning people banding together to work for the community.

Additional brand candidates should remain as backups, but primary MVP brand/domain is now Gadugy / gadugy.com.

## Matching Section

### Built around deeper family alignment.

Gadugy matches families based on the things that actually shape community fit:

- Shared morals and values
- Long-term hopes for your children
- Education philosophy
- Faith and worldview preferences
- Children’s ages
- Local LA community / neighborhood cluster
- Parenting rhythms
- Technology and screen-time attitudes
- Desired community type
- Comfort with AI-assisted or human-reviewed matching

You control how much detail other families can see. Some answers can be used only for matching and kept private from other profiles.

## Safety / Trust Section

### Curated, private, and parent-first.

Gadugy is designed for trust from the beginning.

- Families apply before joining
- Profiles are manually reviewed
- ID verification helps confirm real local parents
- Exact addresses are never shown
- Child names are not required publicly
- Direct messaging opens only after a mutual match
- Families can report, block, or decline any connection

### ID verification copy

To keep this community safe and parent-only, every family is reviewed before joining. ID verification helps us confirm that real local parents are behind each profile. Your ID is never shown to other families.

## FAQ

### Is this only for current homeschool families?
No. Gadugy is for families already homeschooling, preparing to homeschool, or seriously considering alternative education because they want a more values-driven path.

### Is this a dating-style swipe app?
No. This is a curated, trust-first family matching community. The goal is fewer, better-fit connections — not endless browsing.

### Do I have to share my faith publicly?
No. Faith and worldview questions can help with matching, but families should be able to control what is visible to others.

### Why ask about technology?
Families often have strong differences around screen time, AI, devices, and childhood learning. Technology philosophy is part of values alignment.

### When is ID verification required?
We are testing the best timing with early families. The goal is to increase trust and safety without making the application feel invasive.

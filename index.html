In plain words

Gadugy helps homeschool families near you find each other — and become friends.

Think of it like a trusted neighborhood introduction service. You tell us about your family. We carefully match you with other local homeschool families who want the same kind of childhood for their kids. Then you meet at a park and see if it clicks.

🏡

You share your hopes

Tell us about your kids' ages, your homeschool stage, and what kind of family community you're looking for.

🧭

We find nearby fits

We suggest local families who share your values, education hopes, and daily rhythm — explained in plain language.

🌳

You meet in real life

Message only when both families say yes, then meet at a park or library and let the kids play.

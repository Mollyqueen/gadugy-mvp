Meet local homeschool families who share your hopes for your children’s future.

Gadugy is a private, curated matching community for values-driven LA families with children ages 0–9 who want nearby parent friendships, playdates, homeschool community, and deeper alignment around education, character, faith, technology, and family life.

Why this exists

It should not be this hard to find families growing in the same direction.

Facebook groups are noisy. Park days are hit-or-miss. Co-op lists rarely show deeper alignment. Gadugy helps you find local families with more than just similar zip codes.

Built around deeper family alignment

Matching considers the things that actually shape community fit — values, long-term hopes, education philosophy, faith preferences, children’s ages, local area, parenting rhythms, screen-time attitudes, and comfort with AI-assisted or human-reviewed matching.

Privacy principles

Designed to protect families before it grows.

Gadugy should feel thoughtful and protective, not invasive. The early MVP keeps sensitive details private by default while testing what families need to trust a local match.
